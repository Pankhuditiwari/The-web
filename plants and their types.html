Plants And Their Types

We have saw many things about flowers, let us learn some things about Plants.

Herbs

  • Herbs are very small in size
  • Their stem is green in colour
  • They live for a few seasons only
  • Herbs are the most flowering plants

Shrubs

  • Shrubs are smaller in size
  • Their stem is woody and strong
  • They live for a few years only
  • They look like bushes
